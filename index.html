Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

DogMindAI respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information Collection and Use

To provide a better experience while using DogMindAI, we may request access to certain information and device features. This information is used only for the purposes described below.

Phone Number

If you use the Lost Pet SOS feature, you may voluntarily provide your phone number to display it on your missing pet poster. Your phone number is not stored on our servers and is not shared with third parties.

Notifications & Alarms

DogMindAI requests permission to send notifications so you can receive reminders for feeding, walks, medications, veterinary appointments, and other custom pet care reminders.

Social Media Sharing

The Lost Pet SOS feature uses your device's built-in sharing options to allow you to share missing pet posters. We do not access, collect, or store your social media accounts or login credentials.

Local Storage

Your pet profiles, notes, reminders, and scan history are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store this information on external servers.

AI Processing and Data Transmission

Camera & Photo Library Access

DogMindAI requests access to your camera and photo library so you can capture or select images of pets, animals, birds, fish, insects, and plants for AI-powered analysis and identification.

Secure AI Image Processing

When you scan an image, it is securely transmitted to our private server and processed using Google's Gemini API to generate AI-powered results such as species identification, pet mood interpretation, and health-related insights. Images are transmitted solely for processing and to protect our API credentials.

No Image Retention

Images are processed in real time and are not stored, saved, or archived on our servers after the analysis is complete. We do not retain your images once the AI response has been returned to your device.
